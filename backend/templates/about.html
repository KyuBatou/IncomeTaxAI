About ITL AI

Intelligent Legal Research with AI

ITL AI, developed by Income Tax Library, is an advanced Artificial Intelligence platform designed to assist Chartered Accountants, Advocates, Tax Practitioners and Finance Professionals in researching and understanding Income Tax and GST laws quickly and accurately.
The platform analyses a comprehensive database of judicial precedents, statutory provisions, circulars, notifications and rules using advanced Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP) technology. Users can simply ask a legal question and receive instant, structured answers supported by relevant provisions of law and judicial decisions.
ITL AI helps professionals save valuable research time, identify relevant legal authorities and understand complex tax provisions with greater clarity and efficiency.

  • Instant AI-powered answers on Income Tax & GST laws
  • Access to extensive Case Laws, Acts, Rules, Circulars & Notifications
  • Smart legal research designed for CA, Advocates & Tax Professionals
  • Developed by Income Tax Library – trusted legal research platform

AI That Understands Tax Law Like a Professional

ITL AI has been designed specifically for the Indian taxation ecosystem. Unlike general-purpose AI tools, the platform is trained on structured legal databases covering Income Tax and GST jurisprudence, enabling it to deliver contextual, legally accurate and research-oriented responses.

By combining artificial intelligence with curated legal databases, ITL AI assists professionals in identifying relevant statutory provisions, analysing judicial interpretations and understanding the practical application of tax laws.

The platform functions as a smart legal research assistant, helping professionals conduct research faster, verify legal positions and prepare well-informed professional opinions.
